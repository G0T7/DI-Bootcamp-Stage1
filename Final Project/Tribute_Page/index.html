Peter McKinnon

The G.O.A.T of Photography

About:

  • Born on October 28, 1985 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
  • Joined YouTube on February 15, 2010 and uploaded his first video, a vlog in which he visits the popular Kensington Market district of Toronto, on September 23, 2015.
  • Uploaded numerous short films and tutorials. He began his "Filmmaker Reacts!" series in 2019.
  • In 2019 he was named Breakout YouTuber of the Year at the Shorty Awards.
  • Got married in 2020.
  • 2020 received a Streamy for Cinematographer of the Year.
  • Has a Coffee brand called "Peter McKinnon Golden Hour"
